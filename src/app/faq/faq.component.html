What is GL4 Store ?
GL4 Store is a cloud-based gaming library. One of its most popular features is the ability for users to use any computer to play games they buy/download to their Steam accounts. This also allows users to store a large collection of games without using too much computer memory.

Which platforms does GL4 Store support ?
The GL4 Store currently offers PC support. You can check platform compatibility for individual titles by referring to the “About Game” section of any product page.

What’s this about free games ?
GL4 Store will be offering a new free game available each week throughout 2022. When you claim a free game, it’s yours to keep - even after the game is no longer available to new customers for free.

Why does the GL4 Store make exclusivity deals ?
Exclusives are a part of the growth of many successful platforms for games and for other forms of digital entertainment, such as streaming video and music. GL4 works in partnership with developers and publishers to offer games exclusively on the store. In exchange for exclusivity, GL4 provides them with financial support for development and marketing, which enables them to build more polished games with significantly less uncertainty for the creators. In addition, creators will earn 88% of all the revenue from their game, while most stores only offer 70%.
